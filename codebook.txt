Codebook

The subjects are numbered 1-30. No further details on the subjects are given. 

The activities are numbered 1-6. These are 
1 WALKING
2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
4 SITTING
5 STANDING
6 LAYING

There are 180 columns in the tidied data. For ease of recognition the names from the original data are retained
although the actual numbers correspond to means, one mean for each (subject, activity) pair.
